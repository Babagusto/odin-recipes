Chicken Tenders

Description

Succulent, juicy oven fried Chicken Tenders dipped in honey mustard sauce are popular with kids, adults, and make the perfect finger food. No messâ€¦ no fuss, with an irresistible golden crumb!

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • tablespoons lemon juice (or juice of 1/2 a lemon)
  • tablespoon fresh chopped parsley
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt to season
  • 1/4 teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • 28 ounces (800 g) chicken tenders
  • 1 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup regular breadcrumbs (garlic, herb or Italian seasoned for extra flavour)
  • 1 teaspoon mild paprika (or sweet, smoky, spicy)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 400 Degress
  2. Whisk Egg, Oil, Lemon Juice, Parsley, Garlic, Salt, And Pepper
  3. Dip Chicken Tenders Into Mixture and Let Set for 5 Minutes
  4. In another bowl, combine both breadcrumbs , paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and parmesan cheese
  5. Dredge the egg coated chicke into the breadcrumb/parmesan mixture
  6. Arrange tenders on baking sheet/tray and lightly spray with cooking oil spray
  7. Bake for 10 minutes. Flip and bake for another 5-10 minutes or until cooked through
